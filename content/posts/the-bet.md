Like it or not, we appear to be headed towards a future where fully autonomous software development is possible...


...but I haven't always thought we were headed there.

---

In my early computer science classes my professor, a linguist by night, made sure that we understood that natural language was something of a holy grail for computers. He made us attempt to write a program that produced convincing poetry to drive home his point (no one was convinced.)

Just a year after my C++ poetry generator embarrassed itself in-front of the class, the world was introduced to [GPT-2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GPT-2). 

It put our poetry generators to shame. But I didn't see coding agents, I saw software that wrote convincing human language. I knew it was a big deal, and I knew we wouldn't understand the implications for many years.
